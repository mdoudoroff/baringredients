    description

    Vanilla-flavored vodka is generic for vodka which, after distillation is imbued, by infusion, maceration, aging, or secondary distillations, with natural or artificial vanilla flavoring. Noted brand is Stoli Vanil

    flavors
    vanilla, alcohol, spice
