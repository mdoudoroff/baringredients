    sirop de citron

    • non-alcoholic sweeteners /

    • sirop de citron

    description

    Sirop de citron is a French or French-styled lemon syrup, not unlike a lemon version of Rose’s Lime Juice. Common in pre-1950s cocktails, especially in Europe—and in America previous to Prohibition.

    flavors
    citrus, sweet, fruit, lemon
    substitute
    other citrus fruit-flavored syrup
