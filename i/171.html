    synonyms

    melon schnapps

    description

    Melon liqueur is a generic category for liqueur with natural and/or artificial melon flavoring and artificial color. Notable brand is Midori.

    flavors
    melon, sweet, fruit
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Canada, Netherlands, Germany, Australia, Minnesota (United States), Quebec (Canada), Japan
