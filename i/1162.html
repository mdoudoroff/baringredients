    ice cream

    • non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants /
    • ice cream

    description

    Ice cream is generic for the common preparation of frozen milk, cream, sugar, but ambiguous about flavorings. The default may be vanilla ice cream, but that might not be a desirable flavor for a given drink—discretion is required.
