    plum eau de vie

    description

    Plum eau de vie is generic for unaged brandy distilled from any of a number of plum varieties.

    Caveat emptor: eau de vies can vary wildly in quality and characteristics across manufacturers, even if they are derived from the same fruit. Experimentation is often necessary in locating an ideal eau de vie for a given cocktail.
