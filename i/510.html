    synonyms

    apple schnapps, apple-flavored brandy, apple schnapps liqueur

    description

    Apple liqueur is a generic category for liqueurs with natural and/or artificial apple flavoring in various colors and spirit bases.

    flavors
    sweet, fruit, apple
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Germany, Canada, Netherlands, England, Colorado (United States), Pennsylvania (United States), Belgium, Italy
