    description

    Blended whiskey is generic for whiskey produced in various countries that is mixed with grain neutral spirits and bottled. More common in tall drinks (such as highballs) than in cocktails.

    Less commonly in descriptive terminology (“whiskey, a blend”) it can refer to blends of different ages, casks, and/or styles of straight whiskies.
