    description

    Puerto Rican gold rum is generic for the light-bodied, gold-hued rums of Puerto Rico. The color is from a combination of barrel aging and the judicious addition of caramel color.

    flavors
    rum
    substitute
    Other light-bodied brown rum, such as from Cruzan (Virgin Islands) or Flor de Caña (Nicaragua) or Santa Teresa Añejo (Venezuela).
    places (country/region of production)
    Puerto Rico [United States]

    examples

    • Castillo Gold
    • Don Q Gold
    • Ron del Barrilito Three Star
    • Ron del Barrilito Two Star
    • Ron Rico Extra Smooth Premium
