    description

    Ouzo is generic for slightly-sweetened aniseed-flavored Greek aperitif spirit made from pressed grapes, herbs, and berries including aniseed, licorice, mint, wintergreen, fennel and hazelnut. The base is neutral spirits.

    flavors
    anise, alcohol, spice
    places (country/region of production)
    Greece, Wisconsin (United States)
